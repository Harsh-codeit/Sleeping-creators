HOW CONTENT IS GENERATED IN SLEEPING CREATORS
======================================

Sleeping Creators uses AI (Claude by Anthropic) to create social media content
for brands. Here's how it all works in simple terms.


WHAT THE AI KNOWS ABOUT YOUR BRAND
------------------------------------
Before generating anything, the AI is given your brand info:

  - Your brand name
  - Your industry (e.g., fitness, tech, fashion)
  - Your brand voice/tone (e.g., professional, playful, bold)
  - Who your target audience is
  - Your content themes (topics you want to talk about)
  - Your preferred hashtags

This info comes from the client profile you set up in the app.


TYPES OF CONTENT GENERATED
-----------------------------

2. CAROUSEL POSTS (Instagram Slides)

   The AI creates multi-slide Instagram carousels (typically 5 slides).

   How each slide is structured:
     - Slide 1:       A powerful hook that makes people want to swipe
     - Middle slides: Actionable tips or insights (short, punchy text)
     - Last slide:    A call to action ("Follow @yourhandle for more...")

   Each slide is kept under 250 characters. No fluff, high-impact only.

   The slides are then turned into actual images using one of three
   visual templates:

     - Dark Card:     Black background, dark card, light text (sleek look)
     - Floating Card: Cream background, white card (warm, friendly look)
     - Quote White:   Clean white background (minimal, Twitter-style look)

   All images are sized at 1080x1350 pixels (Instagram's 4:5 ratio)
   and use the Sora and Inter fonts. The text size automatically adjusts
   based on how much text is on the slide.


3. BULK GENERATION

   You can generate multiple posts at once across different platforms.
   For example: 2 posts each for LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram =
   6 posts total, automatically spaced 6 hours apart.


HOW AUTOMATED PIPELINES WORK
-------------------------------

You can set up automated content pipelines that run on a schedule:

  1. You configure a pipeline with:
     - Which platforms to post on
     - What type of content (text or carousel)
     - Topics to cover
     - How often to post (e.g., every 6 hours, or at specific times)
     - Whether posts need your approval before going live

  2. The system checks every 5 minutes if any pipeline is due to run.

  3. When it's time, the AI generates fresh content using your brand
     info and the pipeline's topics.

  4. If approval is required:
     - You get a Telegram message with a preview of the post
     - You tap Approve or Reject right from Telegram

  5. If no approval is needed, the post is automatically scheduled
     and published.


HOW PUBLISHING WORKS
----------------------

For Instagram:
  - Text posts are automatically turned into images (using your
    preferred template style) and posted
  - Carousel posts are rendered as multiple slide images and posted
    as an Instagram carousel
  - Hashtags are added to the caption automatically
  - Captions are capped at 2,200 characters (Instagram's limit)

The app uses Instagram's official API to publish everything.


TELEGRAM NOTIFICATIONS
------------------------

The app sends you updates via Telegram:

  - Approval requests with post previews and approve/reject buttons
  - Weekly reports showing:
    - How many clients are active
    - How many posts were published
    - Success rate
    - Total impressions


IN SIMPLE TERMS
-----------------

You tell Sleeping Creators about your brand once. Then it uses AI to write
content that sounds like you, looks professional, and posts it on
your schedule. You can generate one post at a time, generate in bulk,
or set it on autopilot with pipelines. You stay in control through
Telegram approvals or by reviewing drafts in the app.

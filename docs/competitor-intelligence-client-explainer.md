# Competitor Intelligence — How It Works
### A plain-English guide for clients

---

## What Is This Feature?

Sleeping Creators can now watch your competitors for you.

Every week, it automatically looks at the Instagram and LinkedIn pages of brands
you compete with, finds their best-performing posts, and recreates similar content
— written in your brand's voice — and drops it into your content queue for you to
approve and post.

You get the benefit of knowing what's working in your industry without having to
spend hours scrolling through competitor feeds.

---

## How Does the Scraping Work?

We use a tool called **Apify** — a trusted, widely-used data service — to
collect public posts from competitor profiles.

Here's what it collects for each post:

- The caption and text
- The slides (if it's a carousel)
- The hashtags used
- The number of likes, comments, and shares

It only reads **publicly visible** posts. It cannot access private accounts,
Stories that have expired, or any data that isn't already public on the platform.

Think of it like a research assistant who visits a competitor's public page,
reads their posts, and takes notes — but does it automatically and at scale.

---

## How Does Content Get Recreated?

Once the posts are collected, Sleeping Creators scores them by engagement. Posts with more
comments and shares rank higher than posts with just likes (because comments and
shares signal stronger audience interest).

The **top 3 highest-performing posts** each week go through Sleeping Creators's AI.

The AI is given:
- What the competitor posted (the topic, structure, and hook style)
- Your brand profile (your voice, tone, audience, and themes)

It then writes completely **original content** inspired by what worked — same
format, same energy, but fully rewritten for your brand. Nothing is copied.

The result lands in your content queue as a **draft**. You review it, approve it
or edit it, and then it posts like any other piece of content.

## What Does It Cost?

This feature has a small additional cost on top of your Sleeping Creators subscription,
because Apify charges per scraping job.

**Estimated cost breakdown:**

| What | Approximate Cost |
|------|-----------------|
| Scraping 1 competitor profile (up to 50 posts) | ~$0.50–$1.00 per run |
| Weekly automated scan (per competitor) | ~$2–4/month |
| 3 competitors tracked for 1 client | ~$6–12/month |

These are estimates based on Apify's standard pricing. Costs vary slightly
depending on how active a competitor's profile is and how many posts are scanned.

**This cost is separate from your Sleeping Creators plan** and will be itemised clearly
in your monthly invoice.

If you want to keep costs low, you can:
- Limit to 1–2 competitors instead of many
- Run manual scans instead of weekly automated ones

---

## What You're In Control Of

- **You choose the competitors** — your account manager sets up which profiles
  to watch, based on who you tell them to track.
- **Nothing posts automatically** — every recreated piece of content goes into
  your draft queue. You approve it before it ever goes live.
- **You can pause at any time** — competitors can be turned off individually
  without affecting anything else in Sleeping Creators.

---

## In Simple Terms

Sleeping Creators watches what's working for your competitors, figures out their best
content, and writes a version of it for your brand — every week, automatically.
You just review and approve. It's like having a content strategist who never
stops doing competitor research.
